Accordo Partners

Your bridgehead to new markets.

We work closely 'in accord' with clients planning to extend their business operations between North America and Europe in either direction. We bridge the gap between companies in one region and opportunities for trade and investment in the other. We reduce time to market and risk for our client.

Our programs

Our Market Accelerator Programs offer an affordable, customized process for companies entering European or North American markets for the first time or even those with some presence:

  • Immediate representation across European markets
  • Locating commercial, innovation or technology partners
  • Identifying key 'first call' business prospects
  • Generating sales opportunities
  • Event representation
  • Evaluating market potential
  • Extending geographical or sector presence
  • Attracting finance

Accordo Partners offer a tailored, fixed fee, fixed time market entry process. It limits client cost and in-market exposure to risk.
